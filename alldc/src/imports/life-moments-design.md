Design a clean, minimal web application called “Life Moments”.

The purpose of this platform is to allow users to privately record meaningful daily moments in their life. Each moment can have a positive or negative percentage impact on their life progress. The platform visualizes life as a compounding progress score.

General requirements:
- Minimalist design
- Calm, reflective mood
- Works perfectly on both mobile and desktop (responsive layout)
- Private journaling platform (content visible only to the user)
- Soft typography and simple card layouts
- Focus on readability and emotional calmness
- Avoid heavy UI or clutter

Color style:
- White background
- Light gray cards
- Soft accent color (muted blue or soft orange)
- Positive percentage in green
- Negative percentage in red

Typography:
- Clean modern font
- Large readable body text
- Comfortable spacing

Core Concept:
Each life moment contributes a positive or negative percentage to the user's life progress.

Example:
Day 1: progress = 1
Day 2: read a book +0.1% → 1 * (1 + 0.001)
Day 3: drank alcohol -0.005% → 1.001 * (1 - 0.00005)

The progress should feel like a "life score growing over time".
